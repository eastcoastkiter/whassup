Service View:

The service view is the main window for whassup
It's output can be influenced on many settings.

View:
- Warning
Check to see services in warning states

- Critical
Check to see services in critical states

- Acknowleged
Check to see services in which are acknowleged

- Notifications:
Check to see services with notifiction disabled

- Downtime:
Check to see services in Downtime period

You can also change setting in Config Tab
See Config for advice.

If take a look in Help->about you may be able
to see service in OK state too ;o)

Double Click on a service opens Service Detail
Double Click on a host opens Host Detail

With right click will be able to schedule downtimes,
reschedule or acknowledge, if service or host is not Ok.
