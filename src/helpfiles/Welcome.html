Welcome to Whassup Help

Whassup is a monitoring Tool, which will help you to
stay tuned of your infrastructure incidents.

You will need a livestatus socket to use whassup.
Please take a look at Whassup server in this documentation.

Whassup is written in QT (pronounced cute [kju.t]).

For information, please take a look at qt-project.org.

Whassup was started in 2009, upgraded to livestatus and develop since.

For more information on livestatus take a look at (http://mathias-kettner.de/checkmk_livestatus.html)

Whassup is compiled with mingw (Minimalist GNU for Windows).
Please take a llok at (http://www.mingw.org/) too.

The project name is based on "What is up?" and was inspired from a famous beer commercial.
For more on this just search for "wassup - We are not alone"

Whassup is pure Opensource. If you like to change or fork whassup, please feel free
to do so. But please respect the license of the used projects. They did a great job.

If you want to take part in Whassup, please contact me (see about in whassup)
If you have questions I like to recommend http://www.monitoring-portal.org/ for any questions
on monitoring.

Have fun,
